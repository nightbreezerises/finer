2.utils/semantic_similarity.py下代码model = SentenceTransformer(model_name, device=device)加载all-mpnet-base-v2模型报错
报错原因为huggingface无法连接，解决方式参考：https://blog.csdn.net/a61022706/article/details/134887159
设置镜像：export HF_ENDPOINT=https://hf-mirror.com
下载all-mpnet-base-v2模型：
huggingface-cli download sentence-transformers/all-mpnet-base-v2 --local-dir ./all-mpnet-base-v2 --resume-download
下载到/home/hdl/model/all-mpnet-base-v2
添加代码local_model_path = '/home/hdl/model/all-mpnet-base-v2'
model = SentenceTransformer(model_name, device=device)修改为model = SentenceTransformer(local_model_path, device=device)
